Questions for Reflection

For Personal Reflection and Group Discussion
1. How does Gaudium et Spes challenge us to read the "signs of the times" in our own context? What are the most significant signs of the times in our local community and in the global community today?
2. In what ways might our parish or diocese better embody the vision of Church-world relationship presented in Gaudium et Spes? What concrete steps could we take to engage more effectively with the joys and hopes, griefs and anxieties of our neighbors?
3. How does the document's teaching on human dignity challenge specific practices or structures in our society today? Where do we see human dignity affirmed or violated?
4. What aspects of Gaudium et Spes seem most relevant to the formation of priests and religious today? How might this document shape your own ministry?
5. How can we maintain a balance between faithful witness to Catholic teaching and respectful dialogue with those who hold different views? What can we learn from the approach of Gaudium et Spes?
