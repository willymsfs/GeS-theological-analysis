Political Community

• Common Good: "The political community exists for the common good, and not for the benefit of any individual or group"
• Human Rights: Political authority must respect and protect fundamental human rights
• Participation: Citizens have both the right and duty to participate in public life
• Subsidiarity: Higher authorities should not take over what lower bodies can accomplish
• Moral Order: Political authority is bound by the moral order established by God
"The political community exists for the common good: this is its full justification and meaning and the source of its specific and basic right to exist. The common good embraces the sum of those conditions of social life by which individuals, families, and groups can achieve their own fulfillment in a relatively thorough and ready way."
