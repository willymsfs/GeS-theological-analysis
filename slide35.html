Cultural Development

• Human Flourishing: "Culture is essential for the full development of the human person"
• Broad Definition: Culture includes all that helps humans develop their gifts of body and soul
• Historical Dimension: Culture is transmitted through time and represents the collective wisdom of humanity
• Diversity: Recognition and appreciation of diverse cultural expressions
• Universal Access: All people have a right to participate in cultural development
"Man comes to a true and full humanity only through culture, that is through the cultivation of the goods and values of nature. Wherever human life is involved, therefore, nature and culture are quite intimately connected one with the other."
