The Human Person as Focus

"The human person deserves to be preserved; human society deserves to be renewed. Hence the focal point of our total presentation will be man himself, whole and entire, body and soul, heart and conscience, mind and will."
• Integral Vision: Considers the whole person in all dimensions, avoiding reductionism
• Preservation and Renewal: Dual focus on individual dignity and social transformation
• Christ-Centered Mission: "The Church seeks but a solitary goal: to carry forward the work of Christ"
• Service Not Domination: Christ came "to give witness to the truth, to rescue and not to sit in judgment, to serve and not to be served" (Matthew 20:28)
