Paradoxes of Modern Life

• Wealth amid Poverty: "Never has the human race enjoyed such an abundance of wealth, resources and economic power, and yet a huge proportion of the worlds citizens are still tormented by hunger and poverty"
• Freedom alongside Slavery: "Never before has man had so keen an understanding of freedom, yet at the same time new forms of social and psychological slavery make their appearance"
• Unity versus Division: "The world of today has a very vivid awareness of its unity... yet it is most grievously torn into opposing camps"
• Knowledge without Wisdom: "Man painstakingly searches for a better world, without a corresponding spiritual advancement"
"Influenced by such a variety of complexities, many of our contemporaries are kept from accurately identifying permanent values and adjusting them properly to fresh discoveries."
