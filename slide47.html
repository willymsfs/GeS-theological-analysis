The Holy Spirit in the World

• Active Presence: "The Holy Spirit is active in the world, guiding human history toward its fulfillment"
• Universal Work: The Spirit works not only in the Church but throughout creation
• Inspiration: Source of all that is good, true, and beautiful in human culture
• Discernment: Helps believers recognize the signs of the times
• Transformation: Renews the face of the earth and human hearts
"The Spirit of the Lord fills the earth and he who sustains all things has knowledge of every voice. Hence, he guides the people of God, recalling the mystery of Christ and, for believers, penetrates all things and leads them to grasp the inner truth of the world."
