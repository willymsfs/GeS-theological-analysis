The Paschal Mystery

• Central Mystery: "The Paschal Mystery of Christ's death and resurrection is the center of human history"
• Redemptive Suffering: Christ's suffering gives meaning to human suffering
• Victory over Death: Resurrection as the definitive triumph over sin and death
• Participation: Christians are called to participate in Christ's death and resurrection
• Hope: The Paschal Mystery as the source of Christian hope
"Through Christ and in Christ, the riddles of sorrow and death grow meaningful. Apart from His Gospel, they overwhelm us. Christ has risen, destroying death by His death; He has lavished life upon us so that, as sons in the Son, we can cry out in the Spirit: Abba, Father!"
