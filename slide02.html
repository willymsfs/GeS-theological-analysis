Historical Context of Vatican II

• Timeline: Second Vatican Council (1962-1965)
• Pope John XXIII's Vision: "Aggiornamento" - updating the Church to address modern world
• Gaudium et Spes: Final document of the Council, promulgated by Pope Paul VI on December 7, 1965
• Significance: First time Church addressed all humanity, not just Catholics
• Example: Previous encyclicals addressed "Venerable Brethren" while Gaudium et Spes addresses "the whole human family"
