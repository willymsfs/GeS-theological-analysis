Church and State

• Distinct Spheres: "The Church and the political community are autonomous and independent of each other in their own fields"
• Common Service: Both serve the personal and social vocation of the same human beings
• Cooperation: Church and state should cooperate for the common good
• Religious Freedom: The state must respect religious liberty
• Moral Voice: The Church has the right and duty to make moral judgments on political matters when fundamental rights or salvation are at stake
"The Church and the political community in their own fields are autonomous and independent from each other. Yet both, under different titles, are devoted to the personal and social vocation of the same men. The more that both foster sounder cooperation between themselves with due consideration for the circumstances of time and place, the more effective will their service be exercised for the good of all."
