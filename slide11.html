Scientific and Technological Revolution

• Broader Revolution: "Today's spiritual agitation and the changing conditions of life are part of a broader and deeper revolution"
• Scientific Formation: "Intellectual formation is ever increasingly based on the mathematical and natural sciences"
• Cultural Impact: "This scientific spirit has a new kind of impact on the cultural sphere and on modes of thought"
• Technological Transformation: "Technology is now transforming the face of the earth, and is already trying to master outer space"
• Mastery of Time: "The human intellect is also broadening its dominion over time: over the past by means of historical knowledge; over the future, by the art of projecting and by planning"
"The human race has passed from a rather static concept of reality to a more dynamic, evolutionary one."
Scientific & Technological Revolution
Physical World
Transformation of earth's surface, exploration of space, manipulation of matter at atomic level, new energy sources
Social Structures
Urbanization, industrialization, mass communication, global transportation networks, changing work patterns
Human Self-Understanding
Advances in biology, psychology, and social sciences changing how humans understand themselves and their relationships
Philosophical Outlook
Shift from static to dynamic worldview, evolutionary thinking, new questions about meaning and purpose
