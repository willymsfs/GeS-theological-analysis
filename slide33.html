Church's Defense of Marriage and Family

• Advocacy Role: "The Church is called to defend and promote the sanctity of marriage and the family"
• Teaching Authority: Provides clear moral guidance on marriage and family issues
• Pastoral Support: Accompanies families in their joys and struggles
• Social Advocacy: Promotes family-friendly policies in society
• Contemporary Challenges: Addresses modern threats to marriage and family life
"All those, therefore, who exercise influence over communities and social groups should work efficiently for the welfare of marriage and the family. Public authority should regard it as a sacred duty to recognize, protect and promote their authentic nature, to shield public morality and to favor the prosperity of home life."
Pastoral Approaches to Family Ministry
1. PREPARATION
Remote preparation (family of origin), proximate preparation (youth), immediate preparation (engagement)
2. CELEBRATION
Liturgical celebration of marriage as a sacrament and public commitment
3. ACCOMPANIMENT
Ongoing support for newly married couples and families at all stages
4. HEALING & INTEGRATION
Support for families in crisis, separated, divorced, or in irregular situations
5. ADVOCACY
Promoting family-friendly policies and defending family rights in society
