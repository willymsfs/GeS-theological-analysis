Population Growth

• Demographic Challenge: "Many nations face challenges related to population growth"
• Balanced Approach: Need for economic development alongside demographic policies
• Human Dignity: Population policies must respect human rights and dignity
• Moral Methods: Rejection of methods contrary to moral law
• Education: Importance of education about responsible parenthood
"Many are deeply worried by the problem of population growth, whether on a world scale or in the poorer nations. They fear that overpopulation will cause a decrease in world resources and a lowering of the standard of living. Hence, in many countries the public authorities have initiated programs to promote a proper regulation of births. The Council realizes that this involves serious difficulties and problems."
