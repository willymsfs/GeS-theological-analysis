Significance of Gaudium et Spes

• Unprecedented Approach: First time the Church addressed itself to all humanity, not just Catholics
• Methodological Shift: Reading the "signs of the times" and interpreting them in light of the Gospel
• Pastoral Constitution: Combines doctrinal principles with pastoral applications
• Example: Rather than condemning modern errors, it engages in dialogue with contemporary thought
"The council focuses its attention on the world of men, the whole human family along with the sum of those realities in the midst of which it lives; that world which is the theater of man's history, and the heir of his energies, his tragedies and his triumphs."
